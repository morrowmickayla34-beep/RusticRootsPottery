Welcome!

Rustic Roots Pottery welcomes any and all plant or pottery lovers! Our business strives to create the best pottery that suits you, or grow the best plants to fit your pottery pieces.

Pottery

All of our pottery is handmade. We do take commissions and special requests from customers. Rustic Roots Pottery also holds pottery-making events where we provide the material and you provide the talent!

Plants

Alongside our pottery making, we also grow our own selection of plants. If you already have a cozy home available, feel free to browse our wide variety of indoor house plants to fit your needs!
